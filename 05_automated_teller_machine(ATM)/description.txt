EN
-----
An officer wants to withdraw money from an ATM machine. The machine only has 10, 20, 50 and 
100 TL banknotes remain. The number of large denomination banknotes is maximized, 
C, which indicates on the screen how many 100, 50, 20 and 10 lira notes the withdrawal will consist of. 
Write the program.

TR

çekilecek paranın kaç tane 100, 50, 20 ve 10 liralık banknottan oluşacağını ekranda yazan C
